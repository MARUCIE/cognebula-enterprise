# "灵阙财税" (Lingque Finance & Tax Co.) - Product Concept ## Product Metaphor "You're not using a tool — you're hiring a fully staffed AI accounting firm." ## Target Audience Accounting firm managers in China. ## Core Value Proposition A virtual office where AI agents handle complex tax and financial tasks, providing the experience of a premium, fully-staffed firm through an automated interface. ## Proposed Initial Screens 1. **工作台 (Dashboard / Workbench):** A high-level overview of the "AI staff" activity, firm-wide financial health, and urgent tasks. 2. **AI 团队管理 (AI Staff Directory):** A unique view where managers "manage" their AI specialists (e.g., Tax AI, Audit AI, Payroll AI). 3. **客户概览 (Client Portfolio):** A management view of all client accounts being handled by the AI firm. 4. **智能报税任务 (Intelligent Tax Filing):** A deep dive into a specific automated workflow showing the AI's step-by-step progress. 5. **财务报告中心 (Financial Reporting Center):** Where the AI's output (reports, audits) is presented for final human review. ## Visual Direction - **Sidebar:** 240px wide on the left. - **Top Bar:** Company logo + User avatar. - **Theme:** Light theme with #FAFAF8 background, #003A70 primary blue, and #C5913E accent gold. - **Language:** Simplified Chinese.
