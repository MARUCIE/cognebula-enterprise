# PRD: 灵阙智能代账 (Lingque Intelligent Bookkeeping) - Enterprise SaaS Workbench ## Project Overview Lingque Intelligent Bookkeeping is a high-end B2B desktop platform for professional bookkeeping companies. It leverages "AI Digital Employees" to automate 41 monthly accounting tasks across 999+ managed enterprises. The interface must be professional, stable, and highly efficient. ## Target User Professional accountants and bookkeeping firm managers who handle large volumes of enterprise financial data. ## Core Screens to be Designed 1. **AI Operations Dashboard:** High-level overview of automated tasks, AI employee status, and aggregate processing metrics for all 999+ companies. 2. **Enterprise Management Center:** A dense, filterable list of all managed companies with status indicators for their monthly 41-task progress. 3. **Task Automation Console:** A detailed view showing the real-time activity of AI digital employees as they execute specific accounting workflows. 4. **Financial Review Interface:** A professional ledger-style view for human accountants to verify AI-processed data and handle exceptions. ## Design System: "Lingque Office" - **Primary:** Heritage Blue (#003A70) - Symbolizing trust and stability. - **Accent:** Gold (#C5913E) - For professional highlights and status. - **Background:** Cream (#F9F9F7) / Surface: White (#FFFFFF) - **Aesthetic:** Corporate, clean, flat (no rounded corners/shadows), high data density. - **Language:** Chinese (Simplified) + English technical labels. - **Fonts:** Inter & Noto Sans SC.
